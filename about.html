WHO WE ARE?

ABOUT US

Minay Group was established in 2011 as an Oil and Gas drilling and extraction equipment and services company. Our company manufactures equipment in-house and outsources parts for assembly. We also have joint cooperation agreements with reputable petroleum equipment companies. Our service sector includes equipment leasing as well as maintenance and repair.


MINAY GROUP - SHENGJI GROUP Cooperation agreement:

Minay Group holds an exclusive joint-cooperation agreement with Shengji Group. According to the agreement Shengji provides Minay with the most competitive pricing options on products exported to Azerbaijan. Shengji Group is one of the largest Oil and Gas equipment manufacturer within China and is located in the province of Shandong where the second largest oil reserve in China is located, the Shengli Oil Field
